❓ HELP & INSTRUCTIONS

🎯 How to Play

1. Look at the targets that appear on the screen

2. Keep your gaze on each target for the required time

3. The target will disappear when your gaze is registered

4. A new target will appear - keep going!

⚙️ Calibration

Before playing, calibrate the eye tracker:

1. Go to Settings → Eye Tracking Calibration

2. Click "Start Calibration"

3. Look at each point as it appears

4. Aim for 90%+ accuracy for best results

📹 Webcam Setup

1. Ensure good lighting in your room

2. Position camera at eye level

3. Keep face fully visible in webcam

4. Use Settings to adjust mirror/flip if needed

♿ Accessibility

• Enable High Contrast Mode for better visibility

• Adjust display scale if text is too small

• Use Voice Feedback for audio cues

• Keyboard navigation: Tab + Enter

🐛 Troubleshooting

Face not detected: Check lighting and position

Gaze inaccurate: Run calibration again

Camera not found: Check device settings, try different camera

Low FPS: Close other applications, lower resolution
